ShopYouTube

Tagasauris is turning the world's largest video content site into this generation's Home Shopping Network.

Video is Taking Over the World, But Something is Missing
Perhaps Martha Stewart summed it up best at her 2015 Social Media Week interview:

Turning YouTube into The Home Shopping Network

We took Martha's idea to heart by creating ShopYouTube. While still very much at the MVP/prototype stage, ShopYouTube utilizes our A.I. Platform to identify products within any YouTube video, discover them on any shopping portal that's accessible via API (Amazon, Wal-Mart, etc.) and present them to the viewer in a time-synchronous manner.
