The TV Genome

An A.I. engine for video content, built by Tagasauris in partnership with Disney.

If your computer could watch TV, what would it see?
Tagasauris had already built a best-in-class metadata platform when we were contacted by Disney's R&D team. Could we apply that metadata to video, too?

The answer is a resounding yes, and the result is what we call the TV Genome. It's essentially an A.I. enging for video — a tech stack capable of watching TV shows and movies, breaking them down into requisite shots & scenes, and documenting & visualizing the key relationships across the storyline (between characters, themes, locations, music, product placements, and more).

To what end?
Initially, the TV Genome was created as an internal-facing tool for Disney's content creators — a means to interrogate content with unprecedented precision to gain insights on viewing trends, thematic tendencies, and series-long plotlines. But we knew it could be so much more...

The Ultimate Guide to your Favorite Show
Imagine a video-on-deman application that not only let you watch your favorite show, but also provided an infinitely explorable, visual-first deep-dive into the action of the show. Imagine being able to see the relationships between the characters as they grown and twist from episode to episode and season to season. The TV Genome can power that, and so much more.
