An app more than a century in the making.
For more than a century, Bartlett's Familiar Quotations has been known as one of the most carefully curated compendiums of quotes. And, until 2012, the book and its contents were exclusively available in print. With the release of the 18th edition of the renowned collection, Bartlett's decided to bring their beloved tome up to date and onto your mobile device.






This app generates it's own buzz:
Key social networks like Facebook Twitter, Instagram and Pinterest are flooded with quotes-as-images, but no one has had ready access to Bartlett's Familiar Quotations for this purpose, until now. We created Quoto™ for the Bartlett's app — an easy way to make beautiful quote/photo mashups. Every Quoto that gets passed around carries the Bartlett's URL, driving app downloads and drawing in new users. People love to share quotes. And they love to share photos. Put the two together in a manner that's quick and easy to use, and you've got a recipe for app success.
